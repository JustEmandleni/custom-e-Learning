Puberty

Puberty is the period during which males and females reach sexual maturity. Puberty usually begins between the ages of 11 to 15, though it may occur much earlier or later depending on the individual. During puberty the sex hormones are produced which stimulate gametogenesis and sexual maturity. At the same time secondary sexual characteristics develop.
