Precocial and altricial development

Precocial and altricial development are terms used to describe how well-developed offspring are at birth.

Parental Care

In higher-order animals, parental care is a behaviour that increases the survival of the young. As a reproductive strategy, those animals which invest more energy prenatally (before birth) usually display very little parental care once young have been born. In animals where less energy is invested pre-natally, more post-natal parental care is offered.


Parental care can be seen in the following examples:

  • Building of nests and incubation of eggs

  • Guarding from predators

  • Teaching offspring
