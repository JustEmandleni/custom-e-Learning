The outer ear

Figure 18: The inner ear

 

Table 6 below lists the structural adaptations and functions of the various parts of the outer ear.

pinna
  • cartilage flaps situated on the out-side of the ear
  • direct sound waves into the auditory canal
auditory canal
  • tube which passes from the pinna to the tympanic membrane
  • transmits sound waves to the tympanic membrane
  • has little hairs which prevent foreign bodies from entering the ear
  • has wax which prevents the tympanic membrane from drying out
