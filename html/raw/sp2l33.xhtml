Activity 2: Punctuated equilibrium

  1. a) No evolutionary change takes place in phase A ü – the species is in equilibrium with its environment ü.
    b) Phase B points to an accelerated evolutionary change ü due to rapid environmental changes ü.

  2. Punctuated equilibrium ü

  3. During phase B, species with advantageous characteristics, i.e. suitable to the new environment, survive and reproduce. ü Species that do not have advantageous characteristics die out. Thus the chances of speciation during phase B are much greater than during phase A.
