  2. Geotropism is the growth of a plant in response to gravity ü.

  3. The role of auxins in geotropism

  • When the root is placed horizontally, the auxin concentration will be high on the lower side of the root ü
  • gravity attracts auxins ü
  • More growth occurs on the upper side of the root ü
  • because auxins on the lower side inhibit growth ü
  • As a result the upper side of the root grows faster ü
  • causing the root to bend downwards ü
