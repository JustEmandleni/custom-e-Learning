  2. a) Parental care for precocially developed young would be less intensive as these young are already mobile at birth and can feed and fend for themselves   ü  . 
    Parental care for altricial young however would require a greater degree of parental care as these young cannot feed themselves   ü   as their bodies are still under-developed   ü  . Parents would need to feed and protect these young until they reach a more developed state   ü  .
    b) The bodies of precocial young are well developed. Precocially developed young’s eyes are open, they have down feathers or fur and can move soon after birth   ü  . The bodies of altricially developed young however are not as well developed as their eyes are closed, bodies are naked and have very limited movement   ü .
    c) In precocial ovoviviparous species’ eggs, more yolk is found to assist in the development of a well-developed body. This also sustains the young for a longer incubatory period   ü . In altricial ovoviviparous species’ eggs, less yolk is found and the offspring are born sooner, thus being under-developed   ü .
